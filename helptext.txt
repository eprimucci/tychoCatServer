			  HELP
			=======

The aim of this server is provide a collection of objects present in
a sky region at given time.

At the present times you can get stars from UCAC4 catalog, satellites 
or know asteroids.

The url syntax is:

For help (this file):
{server basename}/help  

For stars in UCAC4 catalog:
{server basename}/ucac4?format={output format}&ra={RA degrees}&dec={DEC degrees}&r={degrees}
{server basename}/viz-bin/aserver.cgi?format={output format}&ra={RA degrees}&dec={DEC degrees}&r={degrees}

For Satellites:
{server basename}/sat?format={output format}&date={YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS}&ra={RA degrees}&dec={DEC degrees}&r={degrees}

For asteroids:
{server basename}/mpc?format={output format}&date={YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS}&ra={RA degrees}&dec={DEC degrees}&r={degrees}
{server basename}/mpcsearch?format={output format}&date={YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS}&key={asteroid key}

Implemented {output format} are html,csv,excel or mpc  

NOTES:

{server basename}/viz-bin/aserver.cgi URL is used to mimic CDS aserver output as needed by SCAMP astroref catalog
